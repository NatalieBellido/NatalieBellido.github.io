You're Not Alone

You're Not Alone, 2018

Words of advice from friends and family on how to deal with when life gets tough. The visuals of the video are a sped up recording of another piece I was working on that related to stress and depression. It's called Not Alone and it inspired this piece. Sometimes looking at a picture isn't enough to help comfort someone, so this video's purpose is to help by providing words of advice.
